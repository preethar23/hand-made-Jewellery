Our Story

Artisanal jewellery made with care in our small studio.

100% Made by Hand

At AURA, "handcrafted" isn't just a label—it's our promise. Every single piece of jewellery in our collection is born from a slow, manual process. From the initial sketch to the final polish, we use our hands to forge, bend, and set every detail.

We believe that when a person makes something by hand, they leave a part of their story behind. Our necklaces, bracelets, and earrings aren't mass-produced in a factory; they are individual works of art, each with its own subtle unique character.

Our commitment to manual craftsmanship ensures that your jewelry feels personal, soulful, and truly one-of-a-kind.
